About EMI Calculator
  • EMI (Equated Monthly Installment): Fixed monthly payment including principal and interest
  • Principal: The original loan amount you borrowed
  • Interest: The cost of borrowing money from the lender
  • Amortization: The process of paying off debt over time
  • Prepayment: Paying extra amounts to reduce loan term and interest
  • Affordability: Your ability to comfortably make loan payments
