About Leap Years

Learn about leap year rules and calculations:

  • A year is a leap year if it's divisible by 4
  • Exception: Years divisible by 100 are not leap years
  • Exception to exception: Years divisible by 400 are leap years
  • Leap years help keep our calendar in sync with Earth's orbit
