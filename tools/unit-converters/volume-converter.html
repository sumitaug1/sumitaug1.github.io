Volume Converter

Convert between different units of volume measurement

Common Volume Conversions
  • 1 liter = 0.264172 gallons
  • 1 gallon = 3.78541 liters
  • 1 cubic meter = 1000 liters
  • 1 liter = 1000 milliliters
  • 1 gallon = 4 quarts
  • 1 quart = 2 pints
  • 1 pint = 2 cups
  • 1 cup = 8 fluid ounces
Usage Tips
  • Enter any numeric value in the "From" field
  • Select your input unit from the dropdown menu
  • Choose your desired output unit
  • Click "Convert" to see the result
  • Use "Swap Units" to quickly reverse the conversion
