Convert between different units of pressure measurement

Common Pressure Conversions
  • 1 bar = 100,000 Pa
  • 1 atm = 101,325 Pa
  • 1 psi = 6,894.76 Pa
  • 1 torr = 133.322 Pa
  • 1 mmHg = 133.322 Pa
  • 1 kPa = 1,000 Pa
  • 1 MPa = 1,000,000 Pa
  • 1 bar = 14.5038 psi
Usage Tips
  • Enter any numeric value in the "From" field
  • Select your input unit from the dropdown menu
  • Choose your desired output unit
  • Click "Convert" to see the result
  • Use "Swap Units" to quickly reverse the conversion
