Speed Converter

Convert between different units of speed measurement

Common Speed Conversions
  • 1 km/h = 0.621371 mph
  • 1 mph = 1.60934 km/h
  • 1 m/s = 3.6 km/h
  • 1 knot = 1.852 km/h
  • 1 Mach = 340.3 m/s
  • 1 ft/s = 0.3048 m/s
  • Speed of light = 299,792,458 m/s
  • Speed of sound = 343 m/s
Usage Tips
  • Enter any numeric value in the "From" field
  • Select your input unit from the dropdown menu
  • Choose your desired output unit
  • Click "Convert" to see the result
  • Use "Swap Units" to quickly reverse the conversion
