Data Storage Converter

Convert between different units of data storage measurement

Common Data Storage Conversions
  • 1 KB = 1,024 bytes (Binary)
  • 1 MB = 1,024 KB
  • 1 GB = 1,024 MB
  • 1 TB = 1,024 GB
  • 1 KiB = 1,024 bytes
  • 1 MiB = 1,024 KiB
  • 1 GiB = 1,024 MiB
  • 1 TiB = 1,024 GiB
Usage Tips
  • Enter any numeric value in the "From" field
  • Select your input unit from the dropdown menu
  • Choose your desired output unit
  • Click "Convert" to see the result
  • Use "Swap Units" to quickly reverse the conversion
