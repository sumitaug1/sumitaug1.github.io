Angle Converter

Convert between different units of angle measurement

Common Angle Conversions
  • 1 radian = 57.2958 degrees
  • 1 degree = 0.0174533 radians
  • 1 gradian = 0.9 degrees
  • 1 turn = 360 degrees
  • 1 arcminute = 0.0166667 degrees
  • 1 arcsecond = 0.000277778 degrees
  • 1 turn = 2π radians
  • 1 turn = 400 gradians
Usage Tips
  • Enter any numeric value in the "From" field
  • Select your input unit from the dropdown menu
  • Choose your desired output unit
  • Click "Convert" to see the result
  • Use "Swap Units" to quickly reverse the conversion
