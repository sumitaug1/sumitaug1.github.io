Temperature Converter

Convert between different units of temperature measurement

Common Temperature Conversions
  • 0°C = 32°F (Freezing point of water)
  • 100°C = 212°F (Boiling point of water)
  • 0°C = 273.15K
  • -40°C = -40°F
  • 0°C = 491.67°R
  • 0°C = 0°Ré
  • Triple point of water = 273.16K
  • Planck temperature = 1.41679 × 10^32K
Usage Tips
  • Enter any numeric value in the "From" field
  • Select your input unit from the dropdown menu
  • Choose your desired output unit
  • Click "Convert" to see the result
  • Use "Swap Units" to quickly reverse the conversion
